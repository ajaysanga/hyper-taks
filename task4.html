'RRR' creates history, 'Naatu Naatu' wins Best Original Song

March 13, 2023 08:31 am 
 

Chandrabose and M.M.Keeravani accept the Best Original Song award for 'Naatu Naatu' from 'RRR' onstage during the 95th Annual Academy Awards at Dolly Theatre on March 12,2023 in Hollywood,California.

The win makes 'RRR' the first Indian feature production to win an oscar.

It's time to go Naatu Naatu , India! SS Rajamouli's RRR has created history by becoming thr first Indian feature to win Oscar. The film's 'Naatu Naatu' soundtrack by MM keeravani was awarded the Best Original Song Award at the ongoing 95th Academy Awards in Los Angeles, United States. Composer Keeravani, along with lyricist Chandrabose, collected the award. Keeravani delivered his acceptance speech in the form of a re-written Carpenters song

"I grew up listening to The Carpenters and now here I am with the Oscars. There was only one wish on my mind, so was Rajamouli's and our families' ..... 'RRR' has to win ....Pride of every Indian ....must put me on the top of the world." Keeravani said in tune to The Carpenters' 1970s song Top of the World
